Hummus

Description

This recipe is a traditional chickpea dip. Many different versions of the dip exist, from smooth to creamy, the possibilities are limitless.

In this recipe, I will show how to make a smooth hummus that everyone will enjoy. Feel free to mix ingredients and try your own version.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Ice Water

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight
  2. In the morning, drain and rinse chickpeas
  3. Cook chickpeas with some baking soda
  4. Blend chickpeas with a little cooking liquid, tahini, lemon juice, and salt.
  5. Add ice or ice water until smooth
  6. Serve with warm bread or as a dip for veggies
